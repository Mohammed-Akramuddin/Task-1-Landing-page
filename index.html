Key features of the Flipkart app include:User-Friendly Interface: Easy navigation with categories and search options.Personalized Recommendations: Suggestions based on browsing and purchasing history.Secure Payment Options: Multiple payment methods including UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and COD (Cash on Delivery).Order Tracking: Real-time updates on order status and delivery.Customer Support: Access to 24/7 customer service for queries and complaints.Flipkart Plus: A membership program offering benefits like free shipping, early access to sales, and more.In-App Notifications: Alerts for sales, discounts, and new arrivals.The app is available for both Android and iOS devices, making it accessible to a wide user base.

ABOUT

Flipkart was founded in October 2007 by Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal, both former Amazon employees. It started as an online bookstore and later expanded to offer a wide range of products, including electronics, fashion, home appliances, and more. The Flipkart app, launched to enhance user convenience, allows customers to shop, track orders, and access exclusive deals on their smartphones. Over the years, Flipkart has grown to become one of Indias leading e-commerce platforms.


Evolution of Flipkart

Big Billion Days: Flipkart's "Big Billion Days" sale, which started in 2014, has become one of the biggest shopping events in India, often compared to Amazon's Prime Day and Alibaba's Singles' Day.Acquisitions: Flipkart has acquired several companies over the years, including Myntra, Jabong, PhonePe, and eBay India, to expand its reach and capabilities.Walmart Acquisition: In 2018, Walmart acquired a 77% stake in Flipkart for $16 billion, marking one of the largest e-commerce acquisitions globally.Flipkart Plus: Launched in 2018, Flipkart Plus is a loyalty program that offers benefits like free delivery, early access to sales, and more, similar to Amazon Prime.Technology Innovations: Flipkart has made significant investments in technology, including artificial intelligence and machine learning, to enhance the shopping experience and improve logistics.Video and OTT Content: Flipkart has ventured into video content by launching Flipkart Video, providing users with a range of entertainment options directly within the app.Work Culture: Flipkart is known for its vibrant work culture and has consistently been listed among the top places to work in India.Environmental Initiatives: Flipkart has launched various initiatives to promote sustainability, including reducing plastic use in packaging and promoting eco-friendly products.
